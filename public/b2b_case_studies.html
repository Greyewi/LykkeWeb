Case Studies

Carbon Credit Ecosystem

We are working with Infinite Earth on a pilot program, issuing “Rimba Raya Biodiversity Coins”. Each coin is backed by 22 tonnes of REDD+ credit IOU and protects 1.06 acres of Rimba Raya Biodiversity Reserve for one year, helping preserve the habitat of some of the world’s last remaining orangutans.
