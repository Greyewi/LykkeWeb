How We Do It

  • Business Case

    We first work out the business drivers and identify the areas where tradable tokens can add the most value or save the most money. This includes understanding the market and potentially building models of how people will use the new tokens. 

  • Pretotyping

    Before building anything, we do light and cheap experiments to learn some of the market dynamics quickly. This is very cost-effective and informs our next steps. 

  • Token Design

    We write the specifications for the tokens and how they will bind to rights and obligations in the real world. We have created simple tokens and exotic derivatives. We have experience in collateralization and bond creation, as well as financial engineering of sophisticated market products. 

  • Issuance

    Depending on the tokens, issuance can be simply a matter of sending them to people who will use them, or it can be a complex legal process ending in a sale of assets to the public.

  • Communication & Education

    Projects like these are 20 percent technology and 80 percent business process change. Especially when introducing a new tool, it’s easy to underestimate the amount of effort required. We offer a full suite of blockchain and crypto-educational experiences and materials, to help get your team up to speed and speaking the same language.

  • Token Management

    Often, there are tools that must be built and integrated into your existing systems to create and manage a token ecosystem.

  • Market Making

    In many cases, it’s wise to have a market maker who will purchase tokens and helps build demand for the market. We can partner with you or outside investors for this. 

  • Ecosystems

    The more people who use cryptographic tokens, the more efficient the markets become. After using them in your company for some time, you may want to offer them to other trading partners or even to the public. This could involve creating new types of tokens, derivatives, contracts, wallets, trading desks, analytics, etc. We provide high-performance market tools with a blockchain settlement system.

  • White Labeling

    Our entire Lykke ecosystem — consisting of wallets, exchanges, dashboards, tools, and liquidity providers — can be rebranded and built into any existing system.
