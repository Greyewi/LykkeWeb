What We Do

We offer custom services to build solutions for clients. A few examples: 

01
Settlement coins
A multinational group of companies that do business together across borders may still face days if not weeks to move money from one country to another. Using a common token, they can save on administrative and foreign-exchange expenses and process the p2p-payments faster, better, and cheaper. An example is the exchanging of €, $, £ and other FX payments: in a trading community such payments can be done via settlement coins far more efficiently than through the outdated legacy mechanisms. 
02
Real assets
Most assets in the real world can be represented using blockchain-based tokens that can then be traded. Examples include real estate, land, precious metals, diamonds, credits, ortgages, products with serial numbers, valuable trees, etc. Virtually any assets that can be traded via a contract, can be traded via blockchain-based tokens.
03
Digital assets
Imagine a market where people can actively trade digital game points, loyalty points, airmiles, etc.
04
Economic assets
We are working on a project to create a market for carbon credits. We can also create local currencies and even work with governments to issue fiat cryptocurrencies and bonds. 
05
Tickets
A cryptographic ticket can never be double-sold or double-used. Today, it’s possible to create simple ticket tokens. Soon we’ll be able to make smart tickets that have rules built into them.
